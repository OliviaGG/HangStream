About the platform

Live stream games where chat becomes the players.

HangStream is built for Twitch and TikTok streamers who play games on stream and want their audience to join the action. Viewers can participate from live chat during a stream, or play solo from the game library whenever the streamer is offline.

V

For Viewers

Guess letters, solve words, compete on leaderboards, and practice in solo mode. Viewers can play from chat during a stream or from the browser anytime.

S

For Streamers

Connect Twitch or TikTok chat, show an OBS-friendly overlay, and let the audience become active players during the broadcast.

C

For Communities

Give chat a shared goal. A casual viewer can make a single guess, while regulars build streaks and keep playing between streams.

How It Works
1

Choose a game

Start with Hangman, Spelling Bee, or Speed Scramble. Each game has a solo option for practice and online options for stream participation.

2

Connect the audience

Approved streamers connect supported chat services so viewer messages become game actions. Viewers can also use the browser interface.

3

Show the overlay

The overlay is built for OBS and similar streaming tools, keeping the game visible without covering the creator's main content.

4

Keep play available

Profiles, leaderboards, and solo modes let communities keep playing even when a streamer is not live.

Our Approach

HangStream focuses on clear rules, fast rounds, and respectful community play. Each public page exists to explain the product, help users play, or support streamers running interactive games.
