Support and requests

Need help with HangStream?

Use this page to reach the HangStream team for streamer applications, privacy requests, gameplay bugs, and site issues.

Streamer Applications

If you applied to stream with HangStream, include the email address used on your application and your Twitch or TikTok handle.

Apply to Stream

Privacy Requests

For profile deletion, score removal, or account questions, include the provider you used to sign in and enough detail to find your record.

support@hangstream.me

Bug Reports

For gameplay or overlay issues, include the page URL, browser, device, streamer handle, and what happened right before the problem.

Report a Bug

Before You Contact Us

  • Check the FAQ for common gameplay and account questions.
  • Visit the game library to confirm whether the issue affects one game or every game.
  • For payment or donation problems, do not send card details by email. Include only the date, amount, and email used.
