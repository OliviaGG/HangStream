Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for viewers, streamers, and anyone reviewing how HangStream works before joining a live game.

What is HangStream?

HangStream is a live stream game platform for word games. It lets viewers participate through chat or a browser while the streamer shows the game on an overlay.

Can I play without watching a stream?

Yes. The game library includes solo modes so visitors can practice Hangman, Spelling Bee, and Speed Scramble without connecting to a live broadcaster.

How do chat guesses work?

When a streamer connects supported chat integrations, HangStream listens for eligible messages and turns them into guesses or game actions. The streamer remains responsible for chat moderation and community rules.

Why do viewers sign in?

Sign-in lets HangStream save profile details, scores, and leaderboard history. Public play can still be available depending on the game mode and streamer settings.

What does a streamer need?

A streamer needs an approved account, a browser source for the overlay, and any platform credentials required by the integrations they choose to connect.

Does HangStream store chat messages?

HangStream stores the game data needed for scoring and participation. It does not aim to archive a streamer's full chat history.

Can I request data deletion?

Yes. Use the contact page and include the account email or streamer handle that helps identify the record.

Is HangStream affiliated with Twitch, TikTok, Spotify, or Google?

No. HangStream can connect to third-party services through their APIs, but those services are separate companies with their own rules and policies.
