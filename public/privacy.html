Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data Collection

HangStream collects minimal data necessary for the service to function:

  • Viewer profiles (name, avatar, provider)
  • Game scores and participation history
  • OAuth tokens for authentication (encrypted)
  • Music queue requests

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the server in JSON format. OAuth tokens are encrypted when TOKEN_ENCRYPTION_KEY is configured.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only used to provide the HangStream service.

User Rights

You can request deletion of your data by contacting the streamer or server administrator.

Third-Party Services

When you authenticate through third-party services (Google, Spotify, Twitch, TikTok), those services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.
