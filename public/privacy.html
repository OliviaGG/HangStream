Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what HangStream collects when viewers play games, streamers connect live chat, and users sign in to save profiles or scores.

Information We Collect

  • Profile details from sign-in providers, such as display name, avatar, provider ID, and email when supplied by the provider.
  • Game activity, including guesses, scores, leaderboards, streamer namespaces, and participation history.
  • Streamer application details, including name, email, Twitch or TikTok handle, and application notes.
  • OAuth tokens for connected services when a streamer authorizes integrations. Tokens are encrypted when a token encryption key is configured.
  • Basic technical information such as browser requests, cookies needed for sign-in, and server logs used to keep the service working.

How We Use Information

We use information to run live games, show overlays, save scores, operate streamer dashboards, process applications, keep accounts secure, diagnose errors, and communicate with streamers about their access.

Advertising and Analytics

HangStream may use Google Analytics and Google AdSense on public content pages. These services may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to measure traffic and serve ads. Google explains how it uses information from partner sites at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to operate HangStream, such as authentication providers, streaming platforms, payment processors, email services, analytics, and hosting infrastructure.

Your Choices

You can avoid optional sign-in features by playing public or solo modes where available. You can request deletion or correction of your profile, score, or streamer application data through the contact page.

Children

HangStream is intended for general audiences and stream communities. Streamers should use their platform moderation tools and avoid directing the service to children under 13 without meeting all legal and platform requirements.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards for the size and purpose of the service, including encrypted OAuth token storage when configured and limited administrative access. No online service can guarantee perfect security.
