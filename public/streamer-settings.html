📖 How to stream music

  1. Spotify Premium required - The queue API only works with Premium accounts
  2. Connect Spotify above - Link your account to enable music requests
  3. Keep Spotify open on your streaming computer
  4. Songs auto-add to queue - When viewers type !play song artist, it automatically adds to your Spotify queue
  5. Route audio:
    • OBS: Add → Audio Output Capture → Select your device audio → Enable track for streaming
    • TikTok Live Studio: Add Audio → Select your device audio → Enable microphone/audio
